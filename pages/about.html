About the Club

Our club welcomes students of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned martial artists, who train together in World Taekwondo–style Taekwondo under the guidance of our Masters, coaches, and student leaders. As a competitive Club Sports team, we participate in the Eastern Collegiate Taekwondo Conference and regularly compete in league-sanctioned tournaments hosted at Brown University, Cornell University, Princeton University, and other universities in the region.

In addition to competition, we hold semesterly promotion belt tests sanctioned by World Taekwondo, challenging members both mentally and physically, as well as an annual black belt test for those who demonstrate exceptional skill, leadership, and dedication to the club.
